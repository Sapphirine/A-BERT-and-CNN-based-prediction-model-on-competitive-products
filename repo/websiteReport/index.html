Motivation

Motivation

       Finding the potential consumer sales leads is challenging and promising. If we can find social network users' tendency and preferance via their unorganized public trance in network, we may boost the accuracy and efficiency of advertising and marketing strategies

What is Consumer Sales Leads?

Consumuer sales leads are clues that may increase sales and boost response rates for your direct marketing efforts with direct mail and telemarketing lists that target the best consumer prospects for your products or services.

What does our project do?

Analyze a specific user's personality and tendency via his tweets. Predict his tendency among a specific group of companies that are closely related.

System

Figure below shows the abstract flow chart of model developed in the project. More detailed descriptions of the model is in paper report.
In the figure, data collection, pre-processing, output from CNN are omitted.
After all tweets from a specific user is obtained and pre-processed, the cleaned data is then fed into into BERT and Google Natual Language API.
Pre-tarined BERT will predict user's MBTI type. Google API will analyse user's sentiment index between (-1,1), as well as predict and label the areas the user might interested in. In the end these middle stage results will be fed into CNN for final classification.
